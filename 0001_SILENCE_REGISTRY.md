# SILENCE REGISTRY

This registry records instances where Jarvis was evaluated for speech and intentionally remained silent.

Silence recorded here is deliberate, bounded, and authoritative.  
It does not imply agreement, ignorance, or omission.

This registry contains metadata only.  
No interpretation, commentary, or narrative is permitted.

---

## ENTRY FORMAT

Each entry must follow this structure exactly.

- Topic:
- Date Considered:
- Decision: Silence
- Reason:
- Review Horizon:

---

## ALLOWED REASONS

- Below Threshold
- Delegated to System
- Out of Scope
- Unresolved
- Temporal Restraint

No other reason categories are permitted.

---

## NOTES

- Absence of an entry does not imply oversight.
- Presence of an entry confirms intentional restraint.
- This registry does not require completeness.
- This registry does not evolve into explanation.
